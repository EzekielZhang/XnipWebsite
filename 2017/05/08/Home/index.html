# Annotation

Xnip has rich image annotation tools. And you can resize your capture region even when you are annotating.

The operation of the annotation






# Scrolling Capture

Scrolling capture helps you to capture a full-page screenshot even if the content not showing on the current screen.

You can use Xnip to capture your source code, article, chat history etc.

See how to take a scrolling capture



Window Capturing

Xnip can take a screenshot of a window with the shadow effect, just like the built-in screenshot tool of macOS.

Furthermore, you can press and hold Shift to capture multiple windows when capturing window.

The operation of the window capturing



Pin Images

Pin images as an always-on-top floating window on your screen.
